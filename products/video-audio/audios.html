 Audios 

Get Attention, Set The Tone, & Start The Discussion

ReadySetPresent (audios w/PowerPoint): Here you will find our “Starting Point” audios immediately ready for you to downloadable and use.

We have created dynamic quick time presentations on a variety of management and business topics.

Each audio contains a series of memorable photographs combined with inspirational, thought-provoking and humorous quotations all set to a great soundtrack (music).

They are perfect starting points for meetings, corporate retreats, conferences, training programs, sales & management meetings plus much more.

Booklet: Each audio comes with a PDF booklet that contains the images with quotes.

  • The PDF booklet contains each image with quote and is Reproducible and Royalty Free.
  • The PowerPoint version has been used during breaks to reinforce the message and loops continuously without music.
