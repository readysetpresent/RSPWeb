What This Terms of Use Covers

This policy covers how ReadySetPresent treats personal information collected and received, including information related to past use of ReadySetPresents’ products and services. This policy does not apply to other companies’ practices ReadySetPresent cannot control. Additionally, ReadySetPresent is not responsible for employees it did not employ or manage.

Information Collection and Use

ReadySetPresent collects personal information when you register, visit pages on ReadySetPresent or ReadySetPresent’s partners’ websites, and when you enter promotions or sweepstakes. ReadySetPresent may combine its information about you with information obtained from business partners or other companies.

ReadySetPresent does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies. We only use your information either to provide products or services you have requested, or if we have your permission or under the following circumstances:

  1. 1. We may provide the information to trusted partners, who work on behalf of or with ReadySetPresent under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help ReadySetPresent communicate with you about offers from ReadySetPresent and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.

  3. 3. We may share information if this is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of ReadySetPresent's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  4. 4. We may transfer your information if ReadySetPresent is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, ReadySetPresent will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
