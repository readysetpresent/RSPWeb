Corporate Accounts

A Corporate Account is a special type of member account where credit is granted to a company and ReadySetPresent.com members from that company can purchase products on a company "tab".

Many customers have asked for a corporate account because it allows them to have ReadySetPresent.com directly bill their company monthly via e-mail. It is especially useful for repeated usage or large volume purchases because ReadySetPresent can work with you to fulfill your company’s needs; re-ordering our products will not be necessary.

The ReadySetPresent Corporate Account’s program has been specifically implemented to make the purchasing and licensing of products easier for our valued customers.

We can direct bill you with an invoice based upon your reported monthly usage, or you can purchase additional copies directly through ReadySetPresent.com. Payments are net 30 days. Contact an account representative: sales@readysetpresent.com.

Purchases can be made by filling out our licensing/additional copies PDF document. This document is given to our corporate account clients upon successful implementation of a corporate account. This document can also be requested at any time by emailing sales@readysetpresent.com.
